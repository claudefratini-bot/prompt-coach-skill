# 🧠 Prompt Coach — Claude Skill

A Claude skill that improves your prompts before answering, so you always get the best possible response.

---

## What it does

Instead of answering right away, Claude pauses and asks you 3 quick clarifying questions to understand exactly what you need. It then rewrites your prompt into an improved version and uses it to give you a much better answer.

**The 3 questions:**
1. **Context** — What is your role and your objectives?
2. **Task** — What exactly do you want Claude to do?
3. **Rules & format** — What tone, style, or output format do you want?

---

## How it works

1. You ask a complex question
2. Claude asks the 3 clarifying questions
3. You answer
4. Claude rewrites your prompt into an improved version
5. Claude answers based on the improved prompt

---

## When it triggers

✅ Writing, analysis, strategy, planning, code, business decisions, creative work

❌ Simple factual questions, quick lookups, casual chitchat

---

## Language

Automatically adapts to the language you're using (English, French, etc.)

---

## Installation

Download `SKILL.md` and install it in your Claude skills folder.
